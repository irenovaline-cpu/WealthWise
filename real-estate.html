üí° Key Real Estate Principles

01

Start With REITs Before Physical Property

REITs give you real estate exposure with zero landlord headaches. Learn the asset class before committing to a mortgage.

02

Location Beats Everything

A mediocre property in a great neighborhood outperforms a great property in a mediocre neighborhood every time. Always buy the worst house on the best street.

03

Cash Flow Is King

Never buy a rental property that doesn't cash flow from day one. Negative cash flow properties are speculation, not investing.

04

Build Your Team Early

A great real estate attorney, accountant, and property manager are worth more than any deal. Build these relationships before you need them.
