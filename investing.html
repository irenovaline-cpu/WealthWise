üí° Key Investing Principles

01

Start Before You Feel Ready

The best time to invest was 10 years ago. The second best time is today. Even $50/month invested at 20 beats $500/month invested at 40.

02

Never Try to Time the Market

Studies show that missing just the 10 best days in the market over 20 years cuts your returns in half. Stay invested through volatility.

03

Keep Costs Ruthlessly Low

A 1% annual fee sounds tiny but costs you 25% of your final portfolio over 30 years. Choose index funds with expense ratios under 0.20%.

04

Automate Everything

Set up automatic monthly investments. Remove the decision entirely. Automation is the single biggest predictor of investing success.
