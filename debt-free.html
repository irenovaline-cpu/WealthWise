üí° Key Debt-Free Principles

01

List Every Debt You Owe

Write down every debt: balance, interest rate, and minimum payment. Most people don't know the full picture. Clarity is the first step.

02

Stop Creating New Debt Immediately

You can't fill a bucket with a hole in it. Cut up the cards, freeze accounts, do whatever it takes to stop the bleeding first.

03

Pay More Than the Minimum

Paying only minimums on a $5,000 credit card at 20% APR takes 15 years and costs $8,000 in interest. Double the minimum and you're done in 3 years.

04

Use Windfalls Aggressively

Tax refund, bonus, birthday money ‚Äî every windfall goes straight to debt. This single habit can shave years off your payoff timeline.
